
Hello little explorers! Welcome to Plaza Europa, the heart of Parque Europa. This is a very special square that is dedicated to the European Union, as a tribute to all European countries together!

Do you know what is the most beautiful thing about this square? Which has the flags of the twenty-seven countries of Europe fluttering in the wind. It's like having all of Europe in one place! You can see the flag of Spain, France, Germany, Italy... All together!

In the center of the square there is a very special fountain decorated with a map of Europe. It's like having the entire continent in the water! And on the floor there is a giant map where you can walk over the countries. It's like being giants walking through Europe!

Do you know what the European Union is? It's like a big family of countries that decided to work together to be stronger and help each other. It's like when brothers help each other!

The square also has the flag of the European Union, which is blue with twelve gold stars. It's very pretty! It represents that all European countries are like stars that shine together.

Would you like to walk on the map of Europe? I'm sure you would be very excited! It's like being explorers walking through fairytale countries.

Plaza Europa teaches us that when countries work together, they can achieve incredible things. It's like a big European family!

So enjoy this special square. Maybe it will inspire you to learn more about the countries of Europe and dream of traveling throughout the continent. The European adventure begins here!
